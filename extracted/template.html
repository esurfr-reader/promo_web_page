Why surfers plateau

Most sessions vanish the moment your wetsuit comes off.

You paddle out, you surf, you drive home. No record of where you sat, what you read in the swell, or what you actually did differently. Surf Reader closes that loop — quickly, on the beach, in the language you think in.

The 3-step loop

Map. Plan. Review. Repeat.

One simple loop, repeated every session. The same one Martin teaches at his surf camps — now in your pocket.

1 Map the break

Annotate the lineup with your finger.

Pick from 8 break types — open beach, point, reef, rivermouth, breakwall and more. Drop your "Me" pin, draw the wave direction, mark the rip, place landmarks. Auto-saved as you draw.

2 Set your intention

Guided plan in seven questions.

Where will you sit? How will you paddle out? What's your goal wave count? Surf Reader walks you through Martin's 7-question coaching flow and writes a clean session plan you can save and revisit.

3 Rate your surf

Seven criteria. Five stars each.

From paddle-out to wave selection to bottom-turn execution — score yourself honestly. The app calculates your Skill Rating, charts your progression, and pulls up your map next session so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
